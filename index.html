Hi. I'm Simon.

I'm a lovable Italian Greyhound who likes to sleep, run, and pounce. My beauty and elegance is a source of inspiration. When I am not snuggled under the covers, I am looking cute, asking for food, or doing yoga with my mahm. Namaste.

I was born and raised in southern California. I traveled all over with my mahm. We lived in the desert, mountains and the beach! I loved the mountains the best because I got to walk next to a lake every day. Yippee!

My passion: food, food, and more food. Especially anything duck flavored. I also love soft, squishy, plush toys with squeakers. Mahm thinks I also love sweaters, but I don't really. I love walkies on a bright, clear day!

Fun fact: My original name was Cyrus. But then someone changed it to Simon. I think it suits me quite well. It sounds intelligent, handsome, and whimsical, like me! The most loveable Italian Greyhound on the planet.
